Global Technology Trends

Global hydrogen infrastructure market and technology expansion trends

The global hydrogen market is accelerating infrastructure investment centered on developed countries
such as the US, Europe, Japan, and Australia along with clean energy expansion.

Along with this, demand for AI and digital twin-based smart facility management
and safety & regulation-responsive facility technologies is rapidly increasing.

Key Trends

European REPowerEU & Hydrogen Regulation Enhancement
'Green hydrogen certification standards' are being strengthened according to the REPowerEU strategy, and regulatory framework for hydrogen production methods and usage is being established
Global Hydrogen Port & Charging Network Expansion
Hydrogen ports for hydrogen import/export, hydrogen bunkering, and intercontinental maritime hydrogen distribution networks are expanding
US IRA-based Clean Hydrogen Market Expansion
North American clean hydrogen production and infrastructure investment is surging due to tax benefits through the IRA (Inflation Reduction Act)
AI & DT-based Hydrogen Plant Operation Technology Integration
Intelligent hydrogen facility operation technology integrating AI + Digital Twin + control systems is emerging as core competitiveness
High-pressure & High-capacity Hydrogen Storage & Transport
Tube trailers, liquid hydrogen technology, etc. are evolving toward high pressure (700bar+) and large capacity (10ton+)

Development of 'Export-oriented Hydrogen
Infrastructure Technology' Reflecting Global Trends
