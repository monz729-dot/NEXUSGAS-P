E&M

Convergence of design technology and maintenance expertise, core capabilities for energy infrastructure

NEXUSGAS's E&M (Engineering & Maintenance) division is an integrated facility engineering and maintenance management area
that encompasses design and construction in mechanical, piping, instrumentation, and electrical fields, as well as regular maintenance.

E&M is the core foundation that makes NEXUSGAS's projects and operations sustainable.

Our goal is to simultaneously maximize facility stability and energy efficiency
through data linkage between design and maintenance.
