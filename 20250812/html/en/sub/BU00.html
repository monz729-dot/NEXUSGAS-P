Business

From start to finish of hydrogen infrastructure,
NEXUSGAS stands with you

With integrated capabilities in construction and maintenance covering the entire lifecycle of equipment, we maximize the stability and efficiency of energy infrastructure.

Equipment

Setting the standard for gas infrastructure equipment

NEXUSGAS is a specialized company with infrastructure equipment construction capabilities
for various gas energy sources including hydrogen, ammonia, LNG, and LPG.

We provide comprehensive turn-key services from design to construction and maintenance
across all processes including gas pipeline networks, storage tanks, compressors, and dispensers.

Based on experience from various demonstration projects and industrial sites, we implement
equipment quality that ensures both stability and efficiency.

Technology

Field-centered practical technology, digital-based design and operation expertise

We possess precision construction technology for various environments including high pressure,
ultra-low temperature, and special composite pipes. In the EPC domain, we can provide one-stop
execution from design to field permits, equipment selection and procurement, and process
construction for various projects including gas pipelines, storage tanks, compressors, and
charging facilities. In the O&M (Operation & Maintenance) domain, we establish digital-based
equipment operation and maintenance systems to support stable system operation for customers.
