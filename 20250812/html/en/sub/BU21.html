R&D Projects

NEXUSGAS's R&D vision leading hydrogen-based future energy technology

We are conducting various research and development projects to realize advanced hydrogen energy infrastructure.

From infrastructure technology covering the entire hydrogen storage, transport, and charging cycle to safety control systems and digital twin-based
facility operation technology, we pursue R&D focused on demonstration-type technologies applicable to actual business.

Core Functions

Real-time Site 3D Visualization

Vivid 3D visualization of entire infrastructure
including equipment, piping, and buildings

Sensor/Equipment Status Monitoring

Real-time sensor data such as temperature,
pressure, and flow rate integrated and displayed in 3D

Anomaly Detection and Alarms

Immediate warnings when deviating from normal values,
accident prevention through visual/audio alarms

Process Simulation

Virtual reproduction of process flow from storage
to charging and training support
