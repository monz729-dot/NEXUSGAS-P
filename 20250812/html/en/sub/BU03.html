LNG Infrastructure

Building LNG storage and supply infrastructure based on ultra-low temperature high-pressure technology.

LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) is a high-efficiency clean energy source supplied to major power plants
and industrial facilities nationwide. NEXUSGAS provides comprehensive lifecycle infrastructure from design
to construction and operation based on specialized ultra-low temperature equipment construction technology.

LNG stored at extremely low temperatures below -162°C requires special materials, double insulation design,
and vacuum transfer piping, with advanced safety standards required during construction.

NEXUSGAS has secured total EPC capabilities including high-pressure pump technology, vaporizer modules,
valve stations, and safety instrumentation systems enabling 'alarm → response' functions,
ensuring complete system construction and operational efficiency.

Key Components

  • Enhanced safety with multi-layer insulation design for LNG storage below -162°C
  • Minimized heat loss using vacuum pipelines in high-pressure transfer sections and emergency shut-off capabilities
  • Automatic operation vaporizer enabling stable vaporization under various weather conditions
  • BOG (Boil-Off Gas) Recovery System + Monitoring Module
  • Smart monitoring integrated with boil-off gas recovery system for energy loss prevention and operation optimization
  • Composite material piping for temperature and pressure variations
  • Heat and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Modular structure for improved field installation and maintenance
