ISO Certification

ISO 9001: Quality Management System

"Designing trust through quality – NEXUSGAS's ISO 9001-based quality management"

NEXUSGAS operates a quality management system based on ISO 9001 international standards
to ensure that all products and services provided to customers can consistently meet
certain quality standards. We have established systematic quality processes throughout
the entire process from equipment planning to design, production, delivery, and maintenance,
and are simultaneously realizing quality improvement and customer satisfaction through
regular internal inspections and reviews by external certification bodies.

ISO 14001: Environmental Management System

"Sustainable equipment technology, moving forward with the environment."

As a member of the eco-friendly energy industry, NEXUSGAS has introduced an environmental
management system based on ISO 14001 to periodically analyze and improve the impact of
our overall business on the environment. To minimize the environmental burden that can
occur during the construction and operation of facilities, we maintain a management system
based on the entire process (Plan-Do-Check-Act) of environmental policy establishment →
implementation plan → performance measurement → improvement. This is a core foundation
for moving toward a sustainable company beyond simple legal compliance.

ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management System

"A safe and healthy workplace starts with a systematic system."

NEXUSGAS applies the ISO 45001 occupational health and safety management system to
identify and prevent potential risks in industrial sites in advance. We have an
organizational response system to ensure the safety of all members participating in
construction and operation processes—from field staff to partner companies—and systematically
operate work risk assessment, emergency response procedures, safety education, and accident
prevention activities. This is the foundation for realizing the 'safety first' principle
as an organizational culture, not just simple safety measures.
