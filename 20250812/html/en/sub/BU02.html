LPG Infrastructure

Building stable LPG supply infrastructure for areas without city gas access.

NEXUSGAS establishes stable and economical LPG storage and supply systems for rural areas,
islands, and small industrial complexes where city gas is not available.

We have extensive experience in specialized construction and metering including manganese or
underground storage tank design, meter installation, and small integrated delivery systems.
Our 'Safety Management System' operates in compliance with KGS certification standards.

We support electronic metering-based storage/supply monitoring tailored to customer demand
and remote meter reading functions for vehicles, ensuring both maintenance efficiency and stability.

Key Components

  • Capable of handling various scales from small to medium-large capacity
  • Precise metering and safe piping design application
  • Maximum safety with double sealing structure and backfire prevention valve
  • Multi-fuel mixing capabilities
  • Composite material piping for temperature and pressure variations
  • Heat and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Modular structure for improved field installation and maintenance
