Hydrogen Infrastructure

Building hydrogen-based infrastructure to realize clean energy.

Hydrogen is the key to future energy transition, and the stability and efficiency of infrastructure
throughout the entire cycle from production to storage, transportation, and utilization is crucial.

Based on technical capabilities and performance optimized for hydrogen infrastructure construction,
NEXUSGAS comprehensively handles the design, construction, and operation of each component including piping, storage tanks, compressors, and dispensers.

We meet both safety and scalability requirements demanded by customers through reliable piping material selection in high-pressure hydrogen environments, sensor equipment for leak detection, and remote real-time monitoring system (NEXUS™).

Key Components

  • Application of composite material piping capable of responding to temperature and pressure changes
  • Heat-resistant and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Improved field installation and maintenance through modular structure
  • High-pressure resistant compressor systems supporting 350-700bar operations
  • Multi-layer composite storage tanks with advanced safety features
  • Automated pressure monitoring and control systems
  • High-precision flow measurement and dispensing systems
  • Advanced metering technology for accurate hydrogen delivery
  • User-friendly interface with safety interlocks
  • Real-time monitoring with IoT sensors for leak detection
  • AI-powered predictive maintenance and anomaly alerts
  • Integrated safety shutdown systems for emergency response
