Ansan Hydrogen Pilot City Advanced Pipeline Construction (Section 2-1) / 2.4km
Description This image captures the construction site during the Ansan Hydrogen Pilot City development process, where pipeline construction and hydrogen extractor installation were underway to supply hydrogen to a complex area including 232 rental housing units, sports facilities, and logistics centers. Approximately 8km of pipelines connect above-ground and underground sections, establishing the core supply infrastructure for hydrogen buses and forklift operations.
