3D Digital Twin

The beginning of smart equipment control

NEXUSGAS develops and operates NEXUS™, a 3D digital twin-based control solution that provides
real-time equipment status monitoring, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance (predictive preservation) functions.

By visually integrating the physical location, status information, and real-time data of on-site equipment,
we enhance operational efficiency and incident response capabilities, and deliver core technologies intuitively
through video-based content.
