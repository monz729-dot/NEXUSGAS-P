EPC

NEXUSGAS is a construction specialist company,
consistently performing the Construction (C) domain of EPC projects
focusing on construction in various infrastructure and plant projects.

Based on years of project execution experience and field-centered construction expertise,
NEXUSGAS implements optimal construction strategies tailored to customer needs and provides construction deliverables with assured quality and safety.

We possess specialized construction technologies for high-pressure piping, storage facilities, and fuel equipment,
contributing to business performance with proven technology capable of meeting stringent standards.

Core Capabilities and Implementation

Dedicated Construction Organization Operations

We systematically manage the entire field-centered construction
process by
faithfully reflecting construction quality, safety, and environmental standards.

Customer-Customized Design and Modular Construction Strategy

Through construction standardization and skilled team operations for major facilities
such as storage equipment, piping, and control systems, we deliver construction optimized to customer requirements.

Turnkey Project Execution

We deliver comprehensive lifecycle management
including post-completion commissioning, inspection, and defect remediation with high completeness.

Perfect Safety Regulation Compliance

For high-pressure facilities such as hydrogen, we ensure safe construction
based on construction management systems that reflect both
KGS (national standards) and EIGA/ISO (international standards).

Customer Value

Project Risk Minimization

Integrated management through a single point of contact reduces
field risks such as design omissions, schedule delays, and quality inconsistencies.

Operational Stability Assurance

Faithful implementation of construction standards and safety regulations
guarantees reliability for long-term operation after completion.

Cost Efficiency and Schedule Compliance

We enhance construction efficiency and prevent delivery delays
by coordinating with external factors such as budget and material procurement schedules.
