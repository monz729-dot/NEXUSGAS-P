Core Construction Technology

Precision design, site optimization, construction safety assurance

NEXUSGAS possesses core construction technology capabilities that can respond to
various facility structures and site conditions, based on our expertise in high-pressure gas and hazardous material infrastructure construction.

By applying customized construction methods for each fuel characteristic such as hydrogen, LNG, and LPG,
we overcome construction difficulties according to facility structures and achieve the goals of long-term safety and operational efficiency.

Key Activities

Complex piping structures, high-pressure gas transport lines, etc.
Precision design and layered construction

Hazardous material separation-based unit scenario development and

Various substitute/insulation materials-based extrusion molding bonding construction
technology introduction
