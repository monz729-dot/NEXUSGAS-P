Government Policy Trends

Government policies and industrial strategy trends for hydrogen economy expansion

The hydrogen industry is being fostered as a national strategic industry, and the government is
continuously expanding its mid to long-term roadmap and investment plans.

We are establishing response strategies in line with the following policy directions.

Key Components

The Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy announced the "Hydrogen Economy Implementation Plan" in 2023,
establishing a mid to long-term roadmap covering the entire cycle from hydrogen production-distribution-utilization.
This plan aims to activate the hydrogen industry ecosystem and establish a strategic growth foundation.

CHPS (Clean Hydrogen Portfolio Standard) is a system that mandates power generators to supply clean hydrogen-based electricity above a certain ratio,
which is a key system to drive clean hydrogen power demand and activate the private power generation market.

Safety standards are being strengthened throughout the entire cycle of high-pressure hydrogen facilities,
and the government is promoting public safety through accident prevention, technology standard advancement, and infrastructure expansion
along with the introduction of smart safety management systems.

The government is expanding R&D support for technology demonstration and nationwide deployment of key infrastructure
(charging stations, storage systems, transport equipment, etc.) to realize the hydrogen economy,
with digital-based operation systems and high-performance storage & piping facilities as major support targets.

Hydrogen cities are regional-based hydrogen economy ecosystem models that integrate hydrogen production, supply, and utilization,
and the government provides institutional and financial support for local government-led hydrogen city development
and promotes nationwide hydrogen network expansion.
