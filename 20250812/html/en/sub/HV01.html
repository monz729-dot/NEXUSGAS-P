Hydrogen Value Chain

Hydrogen is connection - NEXUS designs that flow.

The key to realizing the hydrogen economy is 'infrastructure connection'.

NEXUSGAS contributes to realizing the hydrogen economy by building a hydrogen infrastructure Value Chain
that encompasses the entire process of hydrogen production, storage, transportation, and charging.

Features

The hydrogen economy is not an independent technology but a connected flow.

The stages of hydrogen production → storage → transportation → utilization must be connected through
infrastructure such as pipelines, storage tanks, and charging stations to actually work.

NEXUS's role is to technically implement this flow and create infrastructure that can be operated according to field conditions.

NEXUS is a connection designer.

  • We integrate and design pipelines, storage tanks, charging stations, and control systems that comprise the hydrogen value chain.
  • We build infrastructure optimized for each stage and secure safety and efficiency based on field demonstration.
  • Through this, NEXUS is positioning itself as a realization partner for national and regional hydrogen city projects.
