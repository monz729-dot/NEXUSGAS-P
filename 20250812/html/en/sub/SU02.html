Specialized Management Standards by H2/LNG/LPG Equipment

Hydrogen (H₂) Equipment Specialized Management Standards

Item Management Standards Source
Materials/Components Use of hydrogen embrittlement-resistant stainless steel (e.g., 316L) and alloy steel ISO 19880-2:2020
Pressure/Temperature Control Pressure relief valves and dual safety devices for high-pressure hydrogen (up to 700 bar) ISO 19880-1, KGS GH211
Leak Detection Installation of dedicated H₂ sensors with automatic shutdown function upon leak detection KGS GH211, IEC 60079
Explosion-proof Design Explosion-proof rating design compliant with Class 1, Div 2 or Zone 2 KGS GH211, IEC 60079, NFPA 2
Piping Structure Welding method prioritized, minimized flange use (for leak reduction purposes) ISO 19880-2, ASME B31.12
Inspection and Certification KGS high-pressure gas facility inspection, non-destructive testing (NDT), hydrogen station standard compliance KGS GH212, Industrial Safety and Health Standards
