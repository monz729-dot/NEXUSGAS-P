ESG

Our commitment to sustainable energy infrastructure

We realize ESG values through hydrogen-centered low-carbon infrastructure construction and responsible management.

Environmental

"We create a sustainable future through hydrogen-based low-carbon infrastructure."
NEXUSGAS contributes to reducing carbon emissions in the energy industry through hydrogen infrastructure construction. We apply eco-friendly technology in all stages of production, storage, transportation, and utilization,
and continuously introduce facilities and systems that comply with domestic and international environmental regulations and Net-Zero standards.

Carbon Reduction through Clean Hydrogen Supply

  • Building green/blue hydrogen production and supply infrastructure
  • Developing low-carbon emission infrastructure technology
  • Expanding renewable energy-linked hydrogen production systems

Environmental Impact Minimization

  • Environmental impact assessment and mitigation measures for all projects
  • Eco-friendly material and process application
  • Waste reduction and resource circulation system introduction

Social

"We grow together with communities through safe and reliable hydrogen infrastructure."
NEXUSGAS operates the highest level of safety management systems to gain community trust and contributes to regional development through local employment creation and technology transfer.

Safety First Management

  • 24/7 real-time safety monitoring system operation
  • Regular safety training and emergency response system
  • Community safety information sharing and communication

Community Co-prosperity

  • Local employment and supply chain activation
  • Technology transfer and education program support
  • Social contribution activities and community development participation

Governance

"We implement transparent and responsible management through ethical management and governance."
NEXUSGAS establishes sustainable management foundation through transparent decision-making processes, ethical management practices, and stakeholder-centered governance structure.

Transparent Management

  • Regular disclosure of management information and performance
  • Independent board operation and decision-making process
  • Risk management system and internal control strengthening

Ethical Management

  • Code of ethics establishment and compliance program operation
  • Anti-corruption and compliance management system
  • Stakeholder communication and feedback system
