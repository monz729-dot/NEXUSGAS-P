R&D Projects

NEXUSGAS R&D vision leading hydrogen-based future energy technology

We are conducting various R&D projects to realize advanced hydrogen energy infrastructure.

From infrastructure technology covering the entire hydrogen storage, transport, and charging cycle to safety control systems
and digital twin-based facility operation technology, we promote R&D centered on practical application technologies.

Key Components

Develop a platform that collects and analyzes hydrogen station operation data in real-time
to integrate safety, efficiency, maintenance, and customer management

Real-time monitoring of actual operation through virtual models of charging stations and hydrogen infrastructure,
supporting safety and performance simulation for various scenarios

Develop reliable valves and anomaly detection sensors that can operate in high-pressure environments, and automatic shutdown solutions

Build an eco-friendly energy cluster that integrates hydrogen, renewable energy, and fuel cells,
realizing regional carbon neutrality
