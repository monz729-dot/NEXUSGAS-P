Fuel Cell Applications

Final energy conversion, providing hydrogen infrastructure equipment optimized for fuel cells

Hydrogen fuel cells are high-efficiency, pollution-free systems that convert hydrogen into electricity.

As fuel cell applications expand in various environments including residential, industrial, commercial,
and public facilities, building advanced hydrogen-based infrastructure equipment is essential.

Features

NEXUSGAS provides one-stop equipment for the entire process from hydrogen supply, storage, to safety control for fuel cell applications.

We design and deliver customized hydrogen equipment for fuel cells tailored to customers' operational environments, and secure high reliability with technology accumulated in various demonstration sites.

Through this, we support the simultaneous realization of safety assurance for fuel cell business, efficiency improvement of fuel supply, and strengthening long-term operational reliability.
