Equipment Division

LPG Infrastructure

Building stable LPG supply infrastructure for areas not served by city gas.

NEXUSGAS constructs stable and economical LPG storage and supply systems for rural areas,
island regions, and small industrial complexes where city gas is not available.

We have extensive construction and metering experience with specialized manganese or underground storage tank design,
quantifier installation, and small integrated delivery systems, with safety management systems that meet KGS certification standards.

We support electronic metering-based storage/supply monitoring tailored to customer demand and remote meter reading
functionality for vehicles, ensuring both maintenance efficiency and stability.

Key Components

  • Capable of handling various scales from small to medium-large capacity
  • Precise metering and safe piping design applied
  • Maximized safety with double sealing structure and flashback prevention valve
  • Multi-fuel mixing functionality
  • Composite material piping capable of responding to temperature and pressure changes
  • Heat-resistant and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Modular structure for improved field installation and maintenance
