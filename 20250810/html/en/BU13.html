O&M

Convergence of Design Technology and Maintenance Know-how, Core Competency of Energy Infrastructure

NEXUSGAS's O&M (Operation & Maintenance) division is an integrated area
of facility engineering and maintenance management that includes design
and construction in mechanical, piping, instrumentation, and electrical fields, as well as regular maintenance.

O&M is the core foundation that makes NEXUSGAS's projects and operations sustainable.

Our goal is to simultaneously maximize facility stability and energy efficiency
through data integration between design and maintenance.
