LNG Infrastructure

Building LNG storage and supply infrastructure based on ultra-low temperature high-pressure technology.

LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) is a highly efficient clean energy source supplied to major power plants and
industrial facilities nationwide. NEXUSGAS provides comprehensive lifecycle infrastructure from design to
construction and operation, based on our specialized ultra-low temperature facility construction technology.

LNG stored at ultra-low temperatures below -162째C requires special materials, double insulation design, and vacuum transfer piping,
with highly advanced safety standards required during construction.

NEXUSGAS has secured total EPC capabilities including high-pressure pump technology, vaporizer modules, valve stations, and safety instrumented systems
with 'alarm ??response' functionality, ensuring complete system construction and operational efficiency.

Key Components

  • Multi-layer insulation design applied for LNG storage below -162째C, enhancing safety
  • Heat loss minimization using vacuum piping in high-pressure transfer sections and emergency shutoff functionality
  • Automatic operation vaporizer capable of stable vaporization under various weather conditions
  • BOG (Boil-Off Gas) Recovery Device + Monitoring Module
  • Smart monitoring linked with boil-off gas recovery device for energy loss prevention and operation optimization
  • Composite material piping capable of responding to temperature and pressure changes
  • Heat-resistant and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Modular structure for improved field installation and maintenance
