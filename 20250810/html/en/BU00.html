Equipment Division

Setting the standard for gas infrastructure equipment

NEXUSGAS is a specialized company with infrastructure equipment construction
capabilities specialized in various gas energy sources such as hydrogen, ammonia, LNG, and LPG.

We provide full-cycle Turn-Key services from design to construction and maintenance
across all processes including gas pipeline networks, storage tanks, compressors, and dispensers.

Based on experience from various demonstration projects and industrial sites, we implement
equipment quality that secures both stability and efficiency.

Hydrogen Infrastructure LNG Infrastructure LPG Infrastructure

Technology Division

Field-centered practical technology, digital-based design and operation know-how

We possess precision construction technology corresponding to various environments such as
high pressure, ultra-low temperature, and special composite pipes, and are capable of one-stop
execution of the entire process from design to on-site permits, equipment selection and purchase,
and process construction in various projects such as gas piping, storage tanks, compressors and
charging facilities in the EPC area. In the O&M (Operation & Maintenance) area, we establish
digital-based facility operation/maintenance systems to support customers' stable system operation.

Solutions Division

The beginning of smart facility control

NEXUSGAS develops and operates NEXUS?? a 3D digital twin-based control solution that provides
real-time facility status monitoring, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance functions.

It visually integrates the physical location, status information, and real-time data of on-site
facilities to enhance operational efficiency and accident response capabilities, and intuitively
