NEXUSGAS has made it our core value to provide technology and services that customers can trust with confidence, based on our extensive experience and expertise in the gas facility construction sector.

Beyond simple construction, we have continuously contemplated and improved by prioritizing site safety, quality, and customer satisfaction above all else.

Even in the rapidly changing industrial environment, we have successfully carried out various projects based on solid technical capabilities, systematic construction management skills, and flexible and transparent communication. Additionally, we maintain a prepared attitude to become a sincere partner who takes responsibility for post-construction management.

We look forward to continuing meaningful cooperation with your company, and we will become an enterprise that responds with an attitude of doing our best even for small opportunities and delivering results. We hope to contribute together to national industrial development and creating a better energy environment.

NEXUSGAS CEO Yongseok Shin
