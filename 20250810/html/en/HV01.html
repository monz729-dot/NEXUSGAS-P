Hydrogen Value Chain

Hydrogen is connection - NEXUS designs that flow.

The core of realizing the hydrogen economy is 'infrastructure connection'.

NEXUSGAS contributes to realizing the hydrogen economy through building hydrogen infrastructure Value Chain
that encompasses the entire process of hydrogen production, storage, transportation, and charging.

Features

The hydrogen economy is not isolated technology but a connected flow.

The stages leading from hydrogen production → storage → transportation → utilization must be connected through infrastructure
such as piping, storage tanks, and charging stations to actually function.

NEXUS's role is to technically implement this flow and create infrastructure that can be operated to suit the site.

NEXUS is a connection designer.

  • We integrate design piping, storage tanks, charging stations, and control systems that make up the hydrogen value chain.
  • We build infrastructure optimized for each stage and secure safety and efficiency based on field demonstrations.
  • Through this, NEXUS is establishing itself as a realization partner for national and regional hydrogen city projects.
