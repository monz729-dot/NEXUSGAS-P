Hydrogen Infrastructure

Building hydrogen-based infrastructure to realize clean energy.

Hydrogen is the key to future energy transition, and the stability and efficiency of infrastructure
across the entire cycle from production to storage, transportation, and utilization is very important.

NEXUSGAS comprehensively performs design, construction, and operation of each component
including piping, storage tanks, compressors, and dispensers based on technical capabilities and experience
optimized for hydrogen infrastructure construction.

Through reliable piping material configuration in high-pressure hydrogen environments, sensor facilities
for leak detection, and remote real-time monitoring systems (NEXUS™), we simultaneously meet
the safety and scalability requirements demanded by customers.

Key Components

  • Application of composite material piping capable of responding to temperature and pressure changes
  • Configuration of heat-resistant and corrosion-resistant insulation coating system for leak prevention
  • Improvement of on-site installability and maintainability with modular structure
  • Optimized design for high-pressure hydrogen storage (350~700 bar)
  • Integration of multi-layered safety systems including pressure relief devices
  • Real-time monitoring of storage status and automatic response capabilities
  • High-efficiency compression system for various pressure requirements
  • Precision flow control and measurement accuracy improvement
  • User-friendly interface design and operational convenience enhancement
